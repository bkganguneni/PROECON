OUR TEAM

Team consists of well qualified doctorate, master’s degree engineers from IISc, NITs and other reputed universities. Our expertise ranges from the field of Water,Wastewater engineering, Storm water engineering and Structural engineering etc. We are experts in computer-based network modelling of pressurized water networks, sewer networks, storm water and structural modeling & detailing. We also have significant experience in water systems design, GIS and surge analysis. Our software experience include Water GEMS, InfoWater, SynerGEE, Infoworks WS, H2OMapWater, Sewer GEMS, Storm CAD, EPANET,STAAD Pro and Arc GIS etc. Team has worked in the past for several clients such as Welsh Water, Northern Ireland, United Utilities, Southern Water, Thames Water in UK and Middle eastern and Australian projects. Our services include Design of water distribution systems, hydraulic model build and Calibration of DMA’s, water supply zones, Trunk Main analysis, GIS Projects and providing training for various professionals.
